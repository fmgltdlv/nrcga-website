How Committees Work

Committees are volunteer-led groups that drive NRCGA's programs and initiatives.

NRCGA committees are made up of dedicated volunteers from across Nevada's damage prevention community. Each committee focuses on a specific area of NRCGA's mission, from education and training to events and recognition programs.

Committee members meet regularly to plan programs, coordinate events, and develop initiatives that support damage prevention across Nevada. Participation is open to all NRCGA members.

If you're interested in joining a committee, please contact us to learn more about current opportunities and meeting schedules.
