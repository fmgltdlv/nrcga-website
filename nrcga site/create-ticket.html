Create a Ticket

Request utility marking for your excavation project. Call 811 or use the form below to create a locate ticket.

The fastest and most reliable way to create a locate ticket is by calling 811 directly. This form is for informational purposes. For actual ticket creation, please call your local one-call center at 811.

Call 811 at least 2-3 business days before you plan to dig.

What You'll Need

When calling 811 or creating a ticket, have this information ready:

  • Location - Exact address or description of where you'll be digging
  • Type of Work - What you're planning to do (fence, landscaping, construction, etc.)
  • Dig Date - When you plan to start digging
  • Contact Information - Your name, phone number, and email
  • Property Owner - If different from the person calling
  • Marking Instructions - Any special instructions for the locator

The 811 Process

1

Call 811

Contact your local one-call center at least 2-3 business days before you plan to dig.

2

Provide Information

Give the operator your dig location, type of work, and planned start date.

3

Wait for Marking

Utility operators will mark the approximate location of underground lines within 2-3 business days.

4

Verify Marks

Check that all utilities have been marked before you begin digging.

5

Dig Safely

Use hand tools or vacuum excavation within 18-24 inches of marked utilities.
